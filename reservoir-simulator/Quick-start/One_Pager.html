INTERACTIVE 3D RESERVOIR SIMULATION WITH AI-POWERED FIELD DEVELOPMENT PLANNING

Reservoir Simulator delivers an interactive 3D reservoir model with scenario management, well performance analysis, economic evaluation, and SAP ERP integration — calibrated to the Norne field (North Sea) benchmark dataset.

◆ Business Value
  • Optimize $100M+ capital allocation with scenario-based field development planning and NPV analysis
  • Visualize reservoir behavior in 3D with oil/water saturation heatmaps, well trajectories, and layer navigation
  • Compare development scenarios side-by-side with production, economics, and efficiency delta analysis
  • Integrate cost tracking with SAP BDC for CAPEX/OPEX, procurement, and vendor contract management
  • Accelerate reservoir analysis with Foundation Model API-powered AI assistant for optimization
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Expensive simulation software — high-cost commercial tools with steep learning curves
  • Limited scenario comparison — no easy way to compare multiple development plans side-by-side
  • Disconnected economics — reservoir models and financial analysis in separate tools
  • No AI integration — manual interpretation of simulation results without automated recommendations
  • Poor collaboration — desktop-only tools preventing web-based team review of models
