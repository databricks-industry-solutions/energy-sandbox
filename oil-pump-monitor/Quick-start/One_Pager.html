REAL-TIME VIBRATION MONITORING & AI-POWERED FAULT DETECTION FOR FRACKING PUMPS

Oil Pump Monitor delivers live vibration analysis, FFT spectrum visualization, anomaly detection, and an AI operations assistant for upstream oil & gas fracking operations in the North Dakota Bakken Formation.

◆ Business Value
  • Prevent catastrophic pump failures with real-time vibration amplitude and frequency monitoring
  • Identify fault signatures early — bearing faults, cavitation, imbalance, and overspeed detected automatically
  • Reduce unplanned downtime with 2-second refresh live metrics across 6 Bakken field pumps
  • Enable data-driven maintenance with FFT spectrum analysis revealing harmonic patterns
  • Accelerate fault diagnosis with Foundation Model API-powered AI operations assistant
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Undetected vibration anomalies — bearing faults and cavitation causing costly unplanned shutdowns
  • Manual spectrum analysis — technicians interpreting FFT plots without automated pattern recognition
  • No real-time visibility — periodic manual readings missing time-critical vibration excursions
  • Disconnected alerting — sensor alarms not correlated with fault type or recommended action
  • Limited field coverage — monitoring only at the wellhead with no centralized fleet view
