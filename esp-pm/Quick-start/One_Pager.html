END-TO-END ML PIPELINE FOR ESP MONITORING & FAILURE PREDICTION

ESP Predictive Maintenance delivers a complete ML-powered platform — from telemetry ingestion through XGBoost model training, streaming inference, and operational dashboards — to predict and prevent costly ESP failures across upstream artificial lift systems.

◆ Business Value
  • Prevent unplanned failures saving $100K–$500K per workover event in deferred production costs
  • Extend ESP run life with physics-based diagnostics detecting motor overload, gas interference, and bearing wear
  • Optimize maintenance scheduling with AI-driven work order prioritization and SAP ERP integration
  • Improve fleet visibility with real-time health scores and failure risk across 12 wells in 5 basins
  • Accelerate root cause analysis with Foundation Model API-powered troubleshooting assistant
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Reactive maintenance — ESP failures detected only after production impact and costly workovers
  • Fragmented diagnostics — sensor data, work orders, and spare parts in disconnected systems
  • Limited predictive capability — no ML models scoring telemetry in real time for early warning
  • Manual troubleshooting — hours spent diagnosing faults that could be automated with AI
  • Inventory blind spots — spare parts stockouts causing extended well downtime
