ML-POWERED RATE OF PENETRATION PREDICTION & REAL-TIME DRILLING OPTIMIZATION

ROP Prediction delivers an end-to-end ML pipeline — from MSEEL field data ingestion through XGBoost training, Spark Structured Streaming inference, and an operational dashboard — to optimize drilling and reduce well costs by up to $500K+ per well.

◆ Business Value
  • Save $500K+ per well by optimizing rate of penetration through real-time ML predictions
  • Detect drilling hazards early — high MSE, stuck pipe risk, and inefficient drilling flagged automatically
  • Track model performance with MLflow registry showing version history, RMSE, and R² metrics
  • Integrate with SAP ERP for work orders, equipment BOM, procurement tracking, and vendor contracts
  • Enable real-time scoring with Spark Structured Streaming inference on live drilling telemetry
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Suboptimal drilling parameters — no real-time guidance on weight-on-bit, RPM, and flow rate
  • Delayed hazard detection — problems identified only after costly stuck pipe or bit damage
  • No ML in the loop — drilling decisions based on experience alone without predictive models
  • Disconnected data — MSEEL field data, model predictions, and work orders in separate systems
  • Manual performance tracking — no automated comparison of actual vs. predicted ROP
