AI-POWERED AUTONOMOUS SUBSEA INSPECTION WITH COMPUTER VISION & FLEET COMMAND

Subsea Drone Autopilot delivers an end-to-end AI-powered system for controlling a fleet of 5 underwater ROV/AUV drones — from mission planning and real-time computer vision inspection through defect analysis and maintenance recommendations.

◆ Business Value
  • Reduce inspection costs from $50K–$200K per campaign with autonomous AI-planned missions
  • Detect subsea defects automatically with YOLOv8-style CV — corrosion, cracks, marine growth, coating damage
  • Improve safety by replacing manual ROV operations with autonomous drone dispatch and monitoring
  • Accelerate reporting with AI-generated inspection reports including defect cards and evidence frames
  • Enable knowledge-driven operations with RAG-powered Q&A over subsea manuals and fleet history
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Expensive manual inspections — ROV campaigns costing $50K–$200K with slow turnaround
  • Human error in defect detection — manual frame review missing subtle corrosion and coating damage
  • No autonomous planning — mission routing and drone selection done manually without safety optimization
  • Fragmented inspection data — reports, frames, and telemetry in disconnected systems
  • Limited knowledge access — subsea manuals and standards not integrated into workflows
