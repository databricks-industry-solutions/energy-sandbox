REAL-TIME DIGITAL TWIN & AGENTIC AI FOR BLOWOUT PREVENTER SAFETY

BOP Guardian delivers a unified command center that fuses live telemetry, predictive maintenance, SAP ERP integration, and multi-agent AI to protect the most safety-critical asset on any offshore drilling rig — the Blowout Preventer stack.

◆ Business Value
  • Reduce unplanned downtime with AI-driven predictive maintenance and remaining useful life forecasting
  • Accelerate incident response through automated severity-ranked recommendations and crew dispatch
  • Optimize spare parts spend with real-time SAP ERP inventory visibility and min-stock alerts
  • Improve regulatory compliance via continuous monitoring, event logging, and audit-ready dashboards
  • Centralize operations — one platform for telemetry, maintenance, supply chain, and crew management
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Siloed monitoring — sensor data, work orders, and crew schedules live in disconnected systems
  • Reactive maintenance — equipment failures detected only after they impact operations
  • Slow decision-making — manual anomaly analysis delays critical safety responses
  • Certification blind spots — no unified view of crew qualifications for BOP interventions
  • Data latency — batch pipelines miss time-sensitive pressure and temperature excursions
