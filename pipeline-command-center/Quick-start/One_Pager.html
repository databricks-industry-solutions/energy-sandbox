REAL-TIME DIGITAL TWIN & AGENTIC AI FOR MIDSTREAM PIPELINE OPERATIONS

Pipeline Command Center delivers a unified command center for the Eagle Ford Midstream Trunk — fusing live telemetry, predictive maintenance, AI crew allocation, leak detection, and compliance monitoring across 18 assets spanning 87.3 miles.

◆ Business Value
  • Detect pipeline leaks early with dedicated AI leak detection agent analyzing pressure differentials
  • Optimize crew dispatch with skill-based allocation, proximity scoring, and reasoning chains
  • Predict asset failures with RUL forecasting for pipe segments, compressors, valves, and cathodic protection
  • Ensure regulatory compliance with continuous monitoring, event logging, and audit-ready dashboards
  • Centralize pipeline operations — telemetry, work orders, crew, and AI advisors in one platform
◆ Challenges Addressed
  • Leak detection delays — manual monitoring missing time-critical pressure drop events
  • Reactive crew dispatch — no intelligent assignment considering certifications and proximity
  • Siloed asset management — compressor stations, valves, and CP systems monitored independently
  • Integrity blind spots — wall thinning and corrosion detected only during scheduled pigging runs
  • Compliance gaps — fragmented event logs making regulatory reporting difficult
